Specialty Roasts & Asymmetrical Florals

At KR Cafe and Florals, we elevate everyday rituals. Nestled in Jhamsikhel, Lalitpur, our workshop marries the complex art of third-wave specialty coffee brewing with progressive, high-end organic floral designs.

We source single-origin arabica lots grown in Nepal's shade-rich hills of Syangja and Nuwakot, roasting them micro-batch wise to highlight delicate floral notes. Simultaneously, our floral designers design arrangements using indigenous mountain ferns, textured dried elements, and imported premium stems.

Atelier Queries & Answers

Do you deliver bouquets outside of Lalitpur?

Yes, we offer prompt temperature-controlled deliveries across Lalitpur and Kathmandu valleys (inside Ring Road: Rs. 150, outside Ring Road: Rs. 250).

Can I request specific coffee profile beans to take home?

Absolutely. We roast custom profiles weekly. We stock whole-bean bag packs of Syangja and Nuwakot single-origins at our Jhamsikhel counter, grounded to your filter specifications.
