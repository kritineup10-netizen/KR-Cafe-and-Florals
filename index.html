Slowing Down Time in Kathmandu

At **krii**, we believe our daily routines deserve deliberate, peaceful spaces. Nestled away from city noise, we've blended an intimate specialty espresso bar directly with a modern floristry workshop.

Our coffee profile is highly calculated: we select premium arabica micro-lots from across Nepal's highhills, managing custom water mineral profiles for beautifully balanced brews. Simultaneously, our flower team builds architectural, asymmetrical arrangements using native sub-tropical foliage alongside imported stems.
